Overview

Our primary goal for this project was to design and implement a sensor unlock scenario that was secure, creative, and fun! In pursuit of this objective, we designed an unlock method by use of a gyroscope and game display that allows a user to unlock their phone by collecting foods in a particular order predefined by the user. This creative method of unlocking your phone is not only creative but also entertaining and fun.

Gameplay

The game begins with a user selecting that they would like to use the sensor unlock option. The user is then taken to a screen with a face emoji and some food emojis that are placed around the screen randomly. The user must tilt the phone to move the face emoji to the food emojis in the correct order. Once the have been eaten correctly, the phone is unlocked and send to your congratulations screen!

Initial 10 Sketches

Each team member was asked to sketch out 10 ideas that they believe could be suitable for an innovative and engaging unlock application.

Refined 10 Sketches

After agreeing that the "Hungry Man Unlock" idea had the most prospect for creativity and success, each team member created 10 refined sketches that matched their vision of the game. We then discussed all the designs, and decided on the game layout and the feasible features that could be implemented given our short timeline. Here are a subset of sketches that were done during that time.

  1. Fruit Only: A user can choose a series of fruits for the password.
  2. Custom Food Categories: A user can choose a "genre" of foods for the password.
  3. Eating Sounds: The hungry man makes noise when he eats.
  4. Rolling: Hungry man rolls to the fruit when the phone tilts.
  5. Custom man: Make your hungry man custom with hats and spots etc.
  6. Change Background: Users can change the background of their unlock screen.
  7. Hopping: little man hopes to the food when a user tilts the screen.
  8. Incorrect Attempt Cry: Little man is upset when a wrong attempt is made.
  9. Sleeps When Inactive: If the screen has not been tilted in a while, the little man sleeps.
  10. Shaking Phone: If the user shakes the phone instead of tilting it, the little man gets dizzy.
What makes our creative unlock special?

Our creative unlock uses a gyroscope for a method of unlocking a phone. While that is a creative method on its own, integrating gameplay into our unlock method makes our unlock fun as well as functional.
