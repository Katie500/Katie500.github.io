Overview

Our primary goal for this project was to design and implement a gesture unlock scenario that was secure, creative, and fun! In pursuit of this objective, we designed an unlock method by use of a solar system and game display that allows a user to unlock their phone by selecting planets in a particular order predefined by the user. This creative method of unlocking your phone is not only creative but also entertaining and fun.

Gameplay

The game begins with a user selecting that they would like to use the gesture unlock option. The user is then taken to a screen with a solar system displaying some planets that are placed around the screen. The user must tap the planets in their predefined order to succeed. Once the have been tapped correctly, the phone is unlocked and the user is sent to their congratulations screen!

Initial 10 Sketches

Each team member was asked to sketch out 10 ideas that they believe could be suitable for an innovative and engaging unlock application.

Refined 10 Sketches

After agreeing that the "Planet Unlock" idea had the most prospect for creativity and success, each team member created 10 refined sketches that matched their vision of the game. We then discussed all the designs, and decided on the game layout and the feasible features that could be implemented given our short timeline. Here are a subset of sketches that were done during that time.

  1. Unique Planets: Users can name their own planets for unlocking their phone.
  2. Swipe Universe: Users can swipe to connet all their planets together.
  3. Choose the order: Users can choose the order of their planets for the passcode and display.
  4. Stars: Users can integrate stars in place of planets or for added security features.
  5. Custom Look: Users can create a custom look to planets to change their size, shape, color, or design as well as adding moons or rings.
What makes our creative unlock special?

Our creative unlock uses a solar system for a method of unlocking a phone. While that is a creative method on its own, integrating memory patterns into our unlock method makes our unlock fun as well as functional and secure.
