Overview

Elemental Endeavor is a strategic online game that combines the classic gameplay of Tic Tac Toe with elemental powers - Fire, Water, and Earth. Developed as part of the SENG 513 course on October 31, 2023, for Assignment 3, this game challenges players to think strategically and outsmart their opponents in a 5x5 grid.

Gameplay
Objective:

The goal of Elemental Endeavor is to be the first player to connect three of their own elemental tokens in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Winning the Game:

The first player to connect three of their own elemental tokens in a row wins the game. Elemental tokens can form winning combinations horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Game Controls:
  • Use the "Select Your Element" buttons to choose between Fire, Water, and Earth.
  • Click on the cells of the 5x5 grid to place your elemental token.
Modal Instructions:
  • Click on the "Instructions" button to view the game instructions in a modal.
  • The modal provides setup details, gameplay rules, elemental interactions, and winning conditions.
Winner Modal:
  • When a player wins, a modal appears announcing the winner.
  • The modal displays the winning player and provides an option to exit the game.
Exit Button:

Clicking the "Exit" button in the winner modal clears the board and resets the game, returning to Player 1's turn.

Notes:
  • The game incorporates a unique algorithm to handle elemental interactions and prevent infinite loops in token placement.

What Makes This Game Special?

Elemental Endeavor stands out with its unique combination of classic strategy and elemental twists. Here's what makes it special:

  • Strategic Depth: The game introduces a tactical layer with elemental interactions, adding depth to the classic Tic Tac Toe gameplay.
  • Elemental Powers: Players choose from three elements - Fire, Water, and Earth, each with its strengths and weaknesses against the others.
  • Dynamic Gameplay: The ability to replace opponent tokens based on elemental strengths provides dynamic and strategic gameplay.
  • Engaging Visuals: Elemental tokens are visually represented by unique symbols, enhancing the gaming experience.
  • Winning Challenges: Forming winning combinations vertically, horizontally, or diagonally adds an exciting challenge for players.
