About

Craft Metal Products Inc. has provided distinctive lighting since 1955. Since we have had decades of experience in lighting Churches, Libraries, Hotels, Restaurants and other Public Buildings, we have the ability to make a difference for your project. All of our products are fully customizable. We will work to develop a solution that meets your needs and exceeds your expectations! Our lighting team is highly qualified to develop a custom lighting solution to fit every need and budget.
