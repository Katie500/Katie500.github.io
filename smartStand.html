Introduction

In today's fast-paced world, multitasking in the kitchen is more common than ever. However, this often leads to the challenge of efficiently managing cooking tasks while engaging in other activities. This is where the "Whirl-i-Cook SmartStand" comes into play. Our motivation to design and build this innovative device stems from the need to assist busy individuals in their cooking endeavors, making the process smoother and more enjoyable.

Device Overview

The Whirl-i-Cook SmartStand is a state-of-the-art kitchen assistant, leveraging the power of Arduino technology combined with mechanical ingenuity. It is designed to hold a phone or a tablet and intelligently rotate it to face the user, no matter where they move in the kitchen. This allows users to effortlessly follow cooking tutorials, watch their favorite shows, or video call while cooking, without ever losing sight of the screen.

Technical Specifications

The SmartStand is built around the robust Arduino development kit, complemented by:

  • 1x Stepper Motor
  • A set of jumper wires
  • 1x ULN2003 Stepper Motor Driver Module
  • A sturdy base made from recycled materials, that is attached to the stepper motor
Working Principle

The device uses a laptop webcam, coupled with a Teachable Machine model, to detect the user's position in the kitchen. Based on the user's location, it calculates the angle at which the phone/tablet should be rotated for optimal viewing. The stepper motor then precisely rotates the stand to the calculated angle. The servo motors are used to adjust the tilt for better viewing angles, ensuring the screen is always perfectly aligned with the user's line of sight.

User Interaction
  1. Set Up: The user places their phone/tablet on the SmartStand and starts the accompanying software on their laptop.
  2. Automatic Detection: As the user moves around the kitchen, the laptop's webcam, in conjunction with the Teachable Machine model, detects their position.
  3. Dynamic Adjustment: The SmartStand adjusts its orientation in real-time, smoothly following the user's movements.
Impact and Conclusion

The Whirl-i-Cook SmartStand is not just a technological marvel; it's a testament to human-centered design and innovation. It enhances the cooking experience, enabling users to multitask efficiently without missing a beat. Whether it's following a recipe, catching up on a TV show, or simply staying connected, the SmartStand ensures users can do it all while cooking, without ever losing sight of what's important.

As we continue to refine and improve the Whirl-i-Cook SmartStand, we stay committed to our mission of blending technology with everyday convenience, making life a little easier, one rotation at a time.

Integration of Python Script with Arduino for Whirl-i-Cook SmartStand

The Whirl-i-Cook SmartStand is a marvel of modern engineering, seamlessly integrating sophisticated software with precise mechanical control. At the heart of this system lies the synergy between a Python script and an Arduino sketch, working in concert to deliver an unparalleled user experience.

Python Script: The Brains Behind the Operation

The Python script is the central intelligence of the SmartStand. Running on a laptop, it uses the built-in webcam to continually monitor the user's position within the kitchen. The script leverages a Teachable Machine model, a form of machine learning, to intelligently interpret the webcam's visual input. This model has been trained to recognize different positions of the user in the kitchen, labeled from zero to four.

Once the user's position is detected, the script performs a series of calculations to determine the optimal angle at which the stand should be oriented for the user to have the best view of their device. This angle is translated into a specific number of steps that the stepper motor needs to execute to achieve the desired rotation.

The script then sends these instructions to the Arduino board via a serial connection. Each instruction is a command that corresponds to one of the five predefined positions, ensuring that the stand rotates precisely to the angle that aligns the phone or tablet with the user's current position.

Arduino Sketch: Translating Instructions into Motion

The Arduino sketch is designed to receive and interpret the commands sent by the Python script. It acts as the executor, translating the high-level instructions into physical motion.

Upon receiving a command, such as "STEP_ZERO" or "STEP_ONE", the Arduino sketch activates the stepper motor. It precisely controls the number of steps the motor takes, corresponding to the specific position indicated by the command. This control is crucial for ensuring the smooth and accurate rotation of the stand, aligning the device with the user.

Synchronized Functionality for Enhanced User Experience

The combination of the Python script's analytical prowess and the Arduino's mechanical control creates a system that is both intelligent and responsive. This synergy allows the Whirl-i-Cook SmartStand to provide an interactive, user-centric cooking experience. It exemplifies how software and hardware can be harmoniously integrated to create a product that is not only functionally efficient but also intuitive and user-friendly.

In conclusion, the Python script and the Arduino sketch together form the backbone of the Whirl-i-Cook SmartStand, a device that exemplifies the innovative integration of technology into everyday life, making cooking a more connected and enjoyable experience.

DESIGN PROCESS

Our journey in designing the Whirl-i-Cook SmartStand was marked by creativity, collaboration, and innovation. As a team of two, we embarked on this venture with a vision to blend technology seamlessly into the culinary experience.

Initial Ideation and Sketching

Our design process commenced with an initial ideation phase, where both team members, Simon and Katie, sketched out 10 rough design concepts each. This phase resulted in a diverse array of 20 preliminary designs, each presenting a unique approach to integrating technology into the kitchen.

Concept Selection and Development

Among the plethora of ideas, Katie's concept of a track device, designed to ensure the user could always view their phone screen while cooking, stood out. This idea was not only practical in addressing a common kitchen challenge but also presented an intriguing engineering challenge.

The concept envisioned a smart device that would dynamically adjust the position of a phone or tablet, allowing users to effortlessly follow recipes or enjoy entertainment while engaged in culinary tasks. The implementation required a blend of motion tracking, automated mechanical adjustment, and user-friendly design.

A refined storyboard was sketched and a stop-motion video of the problem we were solving solidified the intended use of the device.

Decision and Further Development

After thorough consideration and discussion, we unanimously decided to proceed with the development of Katie's track device concept. This decision was bolstered by the potential we saw in its utility and the engaging challenge it posed in terms of implementation.

We then created a detailed storyboard, outlining the user's journey with the SmartStand, from setting up the device to experiencing its dynamic adjustments during cooking. This storyboard helped us visualize the user experience and refine the design further.

Feedback and Iteration

Presenting our concept and preliminary designs in a class setting, we gathered valuable feedback from peers and instructors. This feedback provided us with critical insights, which we incorporated into subsequent design iterations, further polishing the concept.

Conclusion

The design process of the Whirl-i-Cook SmartStand was a testament to the power of collaborative ideation, iterative design, and user-centric development. From rough sketches to a refined, innovative product, our journey was marked by a steadfast commitment to creating a device that would not only simplify but also enhance the cooking experience.
